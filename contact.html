NOTICE

The International Blockchain Olympiad ("IBCOL") would like to address the spread of misinformation regarding IBCOL and IBCOL in Hong Kong SAR China, the Hong Kong Blockchain Olympiad ("HKBCOL"). Please be cautious of individuals or organisations claiming to represent IBCOL without proper authorisation or documentation. This extends to unsubstantiated allegations against persons affiliated with IBCOL in Hong Kong SAR, China.

All participants of IBCOL are reminded that countries and territories with official organising committees will have the necessary documentation to authorise their representation. Participants must adhere to the processes set forth by the official committees to compete and qualify.

If you are unsure whether a website or communication you have received is legitimate, please contact us.
